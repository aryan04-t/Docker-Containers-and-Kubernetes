# First of all, let's start with Dev Ops:


1. What is DevOps: 


--> DevOps stands for "Development" and "Operations." 

--> DevOps integrates and automates the work of software development and IT operations as a means for improving and shortening the system's development life cycle. 

--> So, basically we can say that DevOps is a set of practices and cultural philosophies that aim to improve collaboration and communication between software development and IT operations teams. 

_ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ 


@ Main Goal of DevOps:

--> DevOps try to enhance efficiency, quality, and overall business outcomes. 

--> And to Improve all these things, the field of DevOps focuses majorly on 2 factors: 
    (i) Enabling faster and more reliable delivery of softwares. 
   (ii) Bridging the gap between development and IT operations.
